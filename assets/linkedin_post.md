🌍 **I built an AI system that analyzes 140 years of Earth's climate data — and the results are alarming.**

After studying Machine Learning through CampusX's 100 Days of ML program, I built an end-to-end Data Science project on one of the most urgent problems of our time: **Climate Change**.

📊 **What the project does:**

✅ Analyzes 140 years of NASA + NOAA temperature records (1880–2024)
✅ Detects historically anomalous years using Z-score statistical method
✅ Clusters 20 countries into Climate Leaders / In Transition / Laggards using KMeans
✅ Classifies country-level climate risk (Stable / At Risk / Critical) using XGBoost
✅ Generates UN-style policy reports using Claude AI
✅ Interactive Streamlit dashboard with 5 analysis tabs

🔥 **What the data revealed:**

→ Earth is 1.4°C hotter than in 1880
→ CO2 has risen 50% — from 280 to 421 ppm
→ 2020s are the HOTTEST decade in recorded history
→ Saudi Arabia emits 16.8 tonnes CO2 per person annually
→ Norway runs on 98% renewable — and emits 60% less CO2
→ Countries with >50% renewable energy emit dramatically less CO2 — THE DATA PROVES IT
